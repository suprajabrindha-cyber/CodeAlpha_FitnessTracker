A sleek and fully functional **Fitness Tracker App** built for the **CodeAlpha App Development Internship** (Task 3). This app helps you monitor your daily fitness activities, log workouts, and visualize your weekly progress — all with a clean, modern interface.

## ✨ Features

- **📊 Dashboard Overview**  
  See your total **workouts**, **calories burned**, **total minutes**, and **active days** at a glance.

- **📈 Weekly Progress Bars**  
  Track your progress toward weekly goals:
  - 🔥 **2,000 Calories** burned
  - 🏋️ **5 Workouts** per week
  - ⏱️ **150 Minutes** of activity

- **➕ Log Workouts**  
  Add new entries manually with:
  - Exercise type (dropdown with icons)
  - Duration (minutes)
  - Calories burned
  - Date picker
  - Optional notes

- **🔍 Filter Your Log**  
  View your workout history filtered by:
  - 📋 **All** entries
  - 📅 **Today**
  - 📆 **This Week**
  - 📊 **This Month**

- **🗑️ Manage Data**  
  Delete individual workout entries or clear your entire log (with confirmation).

- **💾 Local Storage Persistence**  
  All your data is saved automatically in your browser's Local Storage — no sign-ups or servers required.

- **📱 Fully Responsive**  
  Works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

- **🔔 Toast Notifications**  
  Instant feedback when you add, delete, or clear entries.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML5** – Semantic structure
- **CSS3** – Modern styling, flexbox/grid, glassmorphism, animations
- **Vanilla JavaScript (ES6)** – App logic, DOM manipulation, Local Storage API
